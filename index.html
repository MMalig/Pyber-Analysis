Pyber Plot Analysis

In this project, we were tasked with analyzing a set of ride sharing data from a fictioal company known as Pyber. Our findings from the analysis are shown below:

Total Rides and Average Fares by City Type


Most rides occur in urban cities while rural cities hold the highest average fare

% of Total Drivers by City Type


Since most rides occur in urban cities, it makes sense that urban cities will also hold the highest concentration of available drivers

% of Total Fares by City Type


Even though rural cities hold the highest average fare, urban cities still generage the most revenue due to the sheer volume of rides given

% of Rides Fares by City Type


Urban cities account for over two-thirds of the total rides within the dataset
